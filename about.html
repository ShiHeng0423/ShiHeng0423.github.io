About Me

I'm a game developer who loves working on the foundation of games — the systems, mechanics, and tools that make gameplay feel responsive, intuitive, and alive.

My focus lies in gameplay programming and technical design, bridging the gap between creative ideas and technical execution. I enjoy working on systems such as combat flows, AI behaviors, game-state architecture, and engine-level tools that support designers and enhance development workflows.
