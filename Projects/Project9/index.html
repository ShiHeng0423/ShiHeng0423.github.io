A Hero Is Born

Game Overview

A Hero Is Born is a short 2D action-adventure game where you control three distinct heroes in a condensed but complete 8-minute journey to defeat the final boss. Each hero has their own health, mana, combat style, and special abilities.

Players can swap between heroes at any time, unlock skills, and purchase gear from the blacksmith to grow stronger. The game blends fast-paced combat, resource management, and character progression into a compact single-session experience.

Gameplay

You lead a party of three heroes—each with a unique playstyle such as melee or ranged. Strategic character swapping is essential, as each member provides different offensive and defensive options.

Throughout the adventure, players can obtain weapons, armor, and upgrades from the blacksmith, enabling the team to prepare for the climactic boss battle.

Key Features

Three Playable Heroes

Each hero has independent HP, MP, abilities, and combat style.

Character Swapping

Switch freely between heroes at any moment to adapt to different challenges.

Blacksmith Upgrades

Purchase weapons, armor, and upgrades that directly improve each hero's performance.

Short, Complete Adventure

The game is intentionally designed to be completed in around 8 minutes while delivering a full gameplay loop.

Controls

  • Left Click – Attack
  • Right Click – Skill
  • A – Move Left
  • D – Move Right
  • Tab – Swap Character
  • Esc – Pause Menu
  • X – Interact
  • I – Inventory
