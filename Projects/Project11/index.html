Studio Project 3 — Road to Redemption

Overview

Road to Redemption is a 2D survival game inspired by Death’s Gambit. Players progress through procedurally generated levels, defeat enemies, collect upgrades, and race against time to clear the game as quickly as possible while achieving the highest score.

Key Features

  • Randomly generated multi-room maps
  • Four enemy types with distinct AI behaviours
  • Variety of projectile attacks
  • Loot drops and upgrade system
  • Leaderboard with time-based scoring
  • Main Menu, Pause Menu, Win/Lose screens
  • Sound effects and background music

Controls

  • A / D – Move Left & Right
  • Space – Jump
  • O – Open Doors
  • E – Shoot Projectile
  • I – Open Menu / Back
  • Enter – Select

Gameplay Systems

  • Random Map Generator: Linked-list rooms with door-based progression
  • Player Movement: Walking, sliding, crouching with tile-based collision
  • Upgrades: Mini points → Large points for HP, damage, reload upgrades
  • Loot Types: Damage boost, HP regen, upgrade point
  • Projectiles: Fireball, Arrow, Bullet
  • Map Editor: CSV maps with tile IDs, collision, and variants
