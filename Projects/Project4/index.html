GAM 200 & 250

Game Concept

A time-based Grab & Shoot shooter game where the player uses mass-based projectiles to defeat enemies while trapped in an enclosed arena.

These projectiles are the enemies themselves. Players must vacuum them using the GravGrab ability before firing them.

Heavier enemies are more difficult to vacuum and reduce shooting range, but they dealhigher damage or may trigger special effects—making them high-risk, high-reward choices.

Make strategic decisions, use the environment to your advantage, and survive until the timer ends.

Genre: Shooter • 2.5D • Top-Down

Controls

During Cutscenes

Left Ctrl: Skip cutscene

While In Game

W / A / S / D: Player & camera movement

L: Toggle FPS display

Tab: Toggle Hunt List

Left Mouse (Hold): Drag enemy toward the player

Right Mouse: Shoot captured enemy

ESC: Pause / Unpause
