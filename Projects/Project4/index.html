GAM 200 & 250 (Year 2 Project)

Gameplay Video

Game Concept

GAM 200 & 250 is a fast-paced time-based shooter built around a unique Grab & Shoot mechanic. Players use the GravGrab ability to vacuum enemies, turning them into mass-based projectiles.

Heavier enemies are harder to pull in and reduce your firing range — but they hit harder and may trigger powerful special effects. This creates high-risk, high-reward decision-making in every encounter.

Survive waves of enemies, use environmental hazards to your advantage, and manage enemy mass strategically until the round timer ends.

Genre: Shooter • 2.5D • Top-Down

Controls

Cutscenes

  • Left Ctrl: Skip cutscene

In-Game

  • W / A / S / D: Player + camera movement
  • L: Toggle FPS display
  • Tab: Toggle Hunt List
  • Left Mouse (Hold): Drag enemy toward player (GravGrab)
  • Right Mouse: Shoot captured enemy
  • ESC: Pause / Unpause
