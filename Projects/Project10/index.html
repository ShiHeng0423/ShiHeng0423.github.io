Studio Project 4 — AzureVania

Game Overview

Road to Redemption is a 2D co-op puzzle–shooter inspired by Fireboy and Watergirl, MapleStory, and Metal Slug. The game blends cooperative puzzle solving with fast-paced side-scrolling action and interconnected level design.

Two players must work together to trigger mechanisms, unlock new paths, and protect each other as they fight enemies, gather loot, and acquire stronger weapons and armor. The core experience emphasizes coordinated teamwork — “Stronger Together.”

How the Game is Played

Players start in a large map with a short tutorial introducing movement, shooting, and cooperative triggers. Both players begin with an infinite-ammo pistol and must assist one another to access new areas and defeat enemies.

Stronger weapons such as rifles and shotguns are unlocked by solving team-based puzzles and exploring branching paths. NPCs provide hints across the world, while a shop NPC offers equipment and items that significantly boost player stats. Portals act as travel points, sending players into or out of boss arenas.

Armor System

Players can equip armor pieces found or purchased during the game. Wearing a full set of the same tier grants a large bonus based on maximum HP.

Armor Tiers: Ancient > Fight > Jade > Gold > Leather

Controls

  • A / D – Move Left / Right
  • Space – Jump
  • Q – Open Armor Slots
  • E – Open Inventory
  • R – Reload
  • O – Sound Menu
  • F – Interact
  • Left Click – Shoot
  • Right Click – Switch Weapon
